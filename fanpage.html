Still Corners, A Alternative/Indie of the ages

Bio

Still Corners, a alternative/indie band that was created in the year of 2007, Tessa Murray, Lead singer and Keys player, met Greg Hughes,Lead guitarist and producer/engineer, by complete chance at a train stop in london, and ever since they have created some of the best,in my expert opinion, smooth easy listening music to this day. While they may not be the most mainstream bands out there, they are most definetly underrated and a group everyone should know about StillCorners.com
